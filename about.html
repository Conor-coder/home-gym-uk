About Home Gym UK

Empowering UK fitness enthusiasts to build their perfect workout space at home since 2019.

Our Story

Home Gym UK was born from a simple frustration. Our founder, David Mitchell, spent months researching home gym equipment only to find scattered, unreliable information that often didn't apply to UK buyers. After building his own home gym through trial and error, he decided to create the resource he wished had existed.

What started as a small blog in 2019 has grown into the UK's most trusted home fitness resource. We've helped over 50,000 people transform spare bedrooms, garages, and garden sheds into effective workout spaces. Our community spans from complete beginners purchasing their first dumbbells to competitive powerlifters building world-class training facilities.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to quality fitness facilities, regardless of their budget or living situation. Commercial gym memberships can be expensive, inconvenient, and intimidating. A well-planned home gym eliminates these barriers and puts you in complete control of your fitness journey.

Our mission is to provide honest, practical guidance that helps you make informed decisions. We test equipment personally, negotiate exclusive discounts with UK suppliers, and continuously update our recommendations as new products enter the market. Every piece of advice we give is tailored specifically for British homes, budgets, and fitness goals.

What Sets Us Apart

Unlike generic fitness websites, we focus exclusively on the home gym experience. We understand the unique challenges of training at home – limited space, noise restrictions, flooring concerns, and equipment storage. Our team has collectively spent thousands of hours testing products in real home environments, not showrooms.

We also maintain strict editorial independence. Whilst we do earn commission from some product recommendations, we never let this influence our reviews. If a product isn't worth your money, we'll tell you – even if it means missing out on affiliate revenue. Our reputation depends on your trust.

Our Commitment to Quality

Every article on Home Gym UK undergoes rigorous fact-checking and editing. Our equipment reviews involve at least four weeks of hands-on testing. We consult with certified personal trainers, physiotherapists, and strength coaches to ensure our workout programmes are safe and effective.

We're also committed to keeping our content current. The fitness industry evolves rapidly, with new equipment and training methodologies emerging constantly. Our team reviews and updates every article at least annually, removing outdated recommendations and adding new insights.
